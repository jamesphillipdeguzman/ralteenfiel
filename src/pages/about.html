About the Adventure Game

Author: James Phillip De Guzman

Project Milestone: Adventure Game

Game Web App: Based on ralteenfiel2.py (version 2)


Ralteen Fiel's Story

The game is based on a real adventure game I created during my mission, played by the Kiwalan Ward in Iligan, Philippines. This Python adaptation follows the journey of Ms. Ralteen Fiel, who is lost in her dreams. Participants must find 11 Words of Life (WOLs) to unlock the riddle that binds her.


Game Mechanics

Participants begin in the Dark Room, blindfolded, and must find WOLs by engaging with individuals dressed as trees. They must ask, "Are you a Word of Life (WOL)?" and correctly guess the keyword from scripture verses provided. The challenge includes a math game and strategic choices like using a flashlight, a guide, or collecting candy (which can lead to an early game-over).


Progressing Through the Game

  • Stars Room: A resting space after collecting all 11 WOLs.
  • Moon Room: Entered by reciting 3 Nephi 14:7 from the Book of Mormon.
  • Judgment Room: Participants unscramble clues to form a final word.
  • Sun Room: The ultimate reward for completing the game, featuring light, joy, and a message from Matthew 25:21.

The Final Challenge

In the Judgment Room, participants must solve a puzzle: "I perish not, but is infinite..." within 3 minutes. Those who succeed enter the Sun Room, celebrating with friends, leaders, and a jar full of cookies.
